General information

Edge Hill

Located just outside of Ormskirk town centre in the Northwest of England, Edge Hill University is a modern, forward looking institution that has recently invested many millions of pounds in building and providing the highest levels of facilities, including accommodation.

Computing Department

The Computing Department has recently moved (just in time for the start of my first year) to the purpose built Creative Edge building, a photographs of which can be found on the gallery page. I have found the facilities at Creative Edge to be second to none and very much enjoy studying there.
